PhD position

Admissions for 2025 have already closed. We are currently looking for PhD students to for the 2026 entry. School of Computer Science offer multiple PhD scholarships available at the school page and Centers for Doctoral Training. If you are interested, please send an e-mail to Dr Li with the title [Prospective PhD] including (There will be limited positions, for maximizing your chances please apply as soon as possible.):

  • a copy of your CV and explanation for,
  • why you want to work with my group specifically,
  • what topics you would be keen to explore and how would it fit into what we are working on.

    Master/Undergraduate/Interns

    Master and Undergraduate students (including students taking a gap year) are all welcome to apply. Both remote and in-person participation are possible:

  • Make sure that you got permission from your supervisor or you don't have a supervisor.
  • Candidates should have strong coding skills and excellent learning abilities. The internship duration should be at least six months. Please ensure you have sufficient time available during this period and are not engaged in other simultaneous projects. Once prepared, you may submit your application via email.
